About Us

Crafting the Web's
Most Compelling Faces.

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Cape Town, Palisson Technologies is a full-service multimedia studio laser-focused on the end-user experience. We don't just build websites — we architect digital environments people genuinely enjoy inhabiting.

We identified a genuine gap in the Eastern Cape market: small and medium businesses deserving agency-quality digital services but lacking access to them. As commerce migrates online, we believe every business deserves a presence that commands attention and earns trust.

Our ambition extends beyond client work. We're building a platform for collaboration with local developers and designers, creating meaningful opportunities in communities that need them most.
