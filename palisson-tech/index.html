About Us

Palisson Technologies is a multimedia company founded in 2020, currently based in Cape Town, South Africa. Our focus is strictly on the end user side of things, meaning we create nice interfaces for you to look at.

We both code and design. For coding, we use tech like HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React). For designing & prototyping, we use a wide variety of tools such as Figma, Photoshop, InVision, InDesign, Illustrator & Adobe XD.

The drive behind this startup is based off the love of creating clean looking designs and interfaces. A gap was identified in the market, especially in the Eastern Cape, in terms of media and dev houses that cater for small to medium businesses. With the world now going digital, it just seemed like the perfect time to infiltrate a market that is migrating online.

Apart from web development, we also have expertise in branding, UI prototyping, logos and SEO (Search Engine Optimization).

We aim to service small to medium sized businesses by equipping them with the essentials of an online presence.

The end goal for this startup is to become a reputable entity, not just locally but potentially on a national scale.

In the midst of all the potential growth we hope for, we also have aspirations of collaborating with local developers & designers to create job opportunities.
