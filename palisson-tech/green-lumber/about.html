about us

our history

Founded in 2017 in Epping, Cape Town, Green Lumber was created with a mission to produce high-quality products from recycled plastic. If it can be made from wood, we can craft it from recycled plastic, offering a sustainable and durable alternative. Our products are designed for outdoor use, capable of withstanding all weather conditions with little to no maintenance required. They are highly durable, weather-resistant, and insect-proof.

Green Lumber specializes in producing a range of items, including security huts, bespoke furniture, decking, and dirt bin covers, while continuously innovating to meet industry demands. As a 100% BEE-rated company, we take pride in being owned by our dedicated staff and the IDH Foundation, a BEE Trust.

vision and mission

At Green Lumber, we envision a future where sustainable practices are the foundation of every industry. By minimizing our carbon footprint and conserving natural resources, we strive to be leaders in environmental responsibility.


Our mission is to preserve forests through the innovative use of recycled materials in our manufacturing processes. We are committed to providing energy-efficient, cost-effective, and low-maintenance products that meet industry needs, all while keeping our customers at the heart of everything we do.
